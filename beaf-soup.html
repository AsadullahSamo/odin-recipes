Description About Beaf Soup

Vegetable Beef Soup is the perfect cozy soup and such a good way to use up all those veggies stocked in the fridge!

It’s perfectly hearty and filling thanks to the tender chunks of protein-rich beef and abundance of nutritious vegetables. Plus it’s a versatile recipe so feel free to replace veggies with others you may have on hand.


Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless beef chuck roast, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ cup dry red wine (Optional)
  • 3 cups low-sodium beef broth
  • 2 cups cubed rutabaga
  • 1 medium onion, cut into thin wedges
  • 4 carrots, cut diagonally into 1-inch pieces
  • ½ cup thinly sliced celery
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons herbes de Provence

Steps

  1. Cut beef chuck roast into 3/4-inch pieces
  2. Toss together beef, flour, and 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper in a bowl until coated
  3. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add meat and cook until browned, about 5 minutes
  4. Stir in broth, rutabaga, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, bay leaves, herbes de Provence, and remaining salt and pepper
  5. Bring to a boil
  6. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until meat and vegetables are tender, 30 to 40 minutes. Discard bay leaves.
  7. Serve
